Kimchi Jjigae: My Beloved

Explanation

Now, I am not a good cook. I can only cook grilled cheese well, and even then I tend to burn it. But every home cook once burned toast and used bananas in pancake batter for healthiness. Cookies on the other hand, I am a master of cookies. But today I want to challenge myself. Allow me to practice new ways to horrify my stove and make my mother ashamed to call me her daughter.

김치찌개 (kimchi jjigae) is a soup from Korea featuring one of the most common 반찬 (banchan) or Korean side dish. It may or may not be spicy, depending on type of kimchi and peppers used. It may also be vegetarian or vegan depending on kimchi and protein ingredients. For example: kimchi used can include fish sauce and/or shell fish, or can use other ingredients. While I use tuna in my kimchi jjigae, you can use tofu or any other protein such as pork or beef.


Let's Get Into It

Ingredients:

  • A handful of kimchi
  • 2 spring onions
  • A spoonful of 고추장 gochujang paste
  • Two spoons of soy sauce
  • Two regular coffee mug-fuls of water
  • One can of tuna
  • Hot pepper flakes
  • One chili pepper
    • Optional Taste: Fish sauce (if you like fish), more onions, garlic
    • Heat: Use White Kimchi and less spicy peppers. But overall, this is a spicy dish, so hang onto your tastebuds!

Set Up:

  1. Take a handful of kimchi and chop it up
  2. Chop the spring onions into very fine slices
  3. Chop the chili pepper into small pieces, set this aside for later

Soup:

  1. Put the kimchi and onion into a small-medium pot.
  2. Add in the gochujang
  3. Add the soy sauce
  4. Carefully take the lid off the tuna can. Take the lid and press the OIL ONLY from the can into the pot. Set the tuna itself aside for later.
  5. Set the pot onto medium heat for 5-7 minutes, add 2 mugs of water
  6. Add the soy sauce
  7. Add the hot pepper flakes
  8. Stir/mix the soup sides and bottom of the pot

Boiling:

  1. When the kimchi jjigae is to a boil, wait two minutes before adding the tuna and chili pepper.
  2. Boil for one more minute before removing from the heat.

Enjoy!
