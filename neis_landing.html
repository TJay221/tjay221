What is NEIS?

NEIS (the National Education Information System) is what all school staff and faculty use for a variety of purposes. Parents and students even use NEIS. Those of us familiar with PowerSchool… think of PowerSchool on steroids.

NETs typically only use NEIS for logging our personal time, sick time, and business trips as well as checking our paystubs (if your school uploads it).

*NOTE: Your Coteacher needs to contact your school's admin or IT department to set a certificate before you can enter NEIS. SOME CoTs believe NETs have no reason to use NEIS because CoTs can submit our trips. They are incorrect and need to fix themselves.
